‚ùìFrequently Asked Questions

Can I use multiple IDW environments at the same time?

Yes! While only one environment can be in the main window, you can open others in your regular browser alongside Onereach.ai.

Do I need to log in every time I switch environments?

No. Each environment maintains its own session. Once logged in, you stay logged in until you explicitly log out or the session expires.

Can I add custom links that aren't GSX services?

Yes! In the setup wizard, you can add any custom links and assign them to specific environments. They'll appear in the GSX menu.

How do I update the application?

Check for updates via Help ‚Üí Check for Updates. The app will notify you when updates are available.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Each environment and service runs in isolation. Your credentials and data from one environment cannot be accessed by another.

Can I export/import my environment settings?

Your settings are automatically saved. To transfer to another computer, you can copy the configuration files from the application data folder.
